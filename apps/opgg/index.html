    About This Web App

    Data Source

    This web application is based on data from the Ontario Rock Garden & Hardy Plant Society's Germination Guide. The society has compiled extensive data on germination requirements for various plant species, particularly those relevant to rock gardens and hardy plants suitable for Ontario's climate.

    The data includes specific germination codes, viability information, and special handling instructions that help gardeners achieve successful germination.

    Visit the original source: Ontario Rock Garden & Hardy Plant Society Germination Guide

    How to Use This Guide

    This web app provides a user-friendly interface to access the society's germination data:

    • Use the Search Plants tab to find specific plants and view their germination requirements
    • Refer to the Germination Codes tab to understand the letter and number codes used throughout the guide
    • Some plants may have special requirements indicated by symbols like * or !

    Understanding the codes will help you provide the right conditions for successful seed germination.
