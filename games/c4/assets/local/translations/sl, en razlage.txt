4 IN A ROW ON A 7X7 FIELD
Game 4 in a row is a game for two players which takes place on a 7x7 grid and each of the players has its own symbol x or o. During the game, you can give your opponent less chance to place the x or o symbol by coloring the blank fields to block them, but this can only be done after the fourth move and only twice during the game. When one of the players manages to place four symbols horizontally, vertically or diagonally, the loser can continue playing the game so that he can place 2 (if first on the move) or 3 (if second on the move) his symbols anywhere on the grid. The winner is the one who has more connected four symbols in a row on the grid.
